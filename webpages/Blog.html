The internet and digital Medias

Digital media is a digitalized content that can be transmitted over the internet or other computer networks. It can be text, audio, video or other graphical images. It can include a broad cast over the tv network, a blog website or something like that. The internet is WAN (network of networks) that allows us to connect across the world.

Why should you join the internet and use digital medias

Currently, we live in an era which values time more than anything. Here is where the internet comes into advantage. It is one of the fastest ways to transfer data. It also decreases the cost of addressing people. In addition it allows us to widen our addressability. Not only this but also it provides a lot to the environment since it stops the use of physical materials like papers.
